FAQ:

What is expoventure?

Expoventure is where the Vision & Innovation Club gives younger engineers the opportunity to show, promote and finance their projects by connecting them with interested investors and companies' representatives.

How does the selection process work?

Out team will be looking for 20 of the most innovative projects based on originality and project's market value, so you will bw asked in the form to describe it in details.

What about the big day?

Each project will have 15 minutes to be presented, followed by talking session with investors.
